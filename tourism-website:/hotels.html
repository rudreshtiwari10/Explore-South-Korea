1. The Shilla Seoul

The Shilla Hotel Seoul is a luxurious 5-star hotel located in the heart of Seoul, South Korea. It offers a variety of amenities, including a spa, a fitness center, and several restaurants. The hotel is also known for its excellent service and its stunning views of the city.

2. Lotte Hotel Jeju

Lotte Hotel Jeju is a luxurious resort hotel located on the beautiful island of Jeju, South Korea. It offers a variety of amenities, including a spa, a golf course, and several restaurants. The hotel is also known for its stunning views of the ocean and its proximity to many of Jeju's top attractions..

3. Paradise Hotel Busan

Paradise Hotel Busan is a luxurious beachfront hotel located in the city of Busan, South Korea. It offers a variety of amenities, including a casino, a spa, and several restaurants. The hotel is also known for its stunning views of the ocean and its proximity to many of Busan's top attractions.
